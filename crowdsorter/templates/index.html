What is Crowd Sorter?

Crowd Sorter is a platform to build collections of items to be voted on and ranked by the audience of your choosing.

What is a collection?

How does voting work?

Share the link to your collection with whomever you want (or keep it private). Anyone voting on your collection with be shown a pair of items. After selecting a winner, two more items appear.

How does scoring work?

The winner of each pair gets a point and its opponent loses a point. Some points are also awarded for inferred wins on pairs that have not yet been voted on. Items with the least confidence score are prioritized for voting.
