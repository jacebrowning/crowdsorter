Crowd Sorter

Crowdsourced list sorting.

Crowd Sorter is a platform to build collections of items to be voted on and ranked by the audience of your choosing. Similar to Kittenwar!, Hot or Not, and Tinder, users apply a subjective ranking based on the criteria of their choosing, but we give you full control over your data.

What is a collection?

How does voting work?

Share the link to your collection with whomever you want (or keep it private). Anyone voting on your collection with be shown a pair of items. After selecting a winner, two more items appear. Voters can also pass if they are unfamiliar with both items.

How does scoring work?

The winner of each pair gets a point and its opponent loses a point. Some points are also awarded for inferred wins on pairs that have not yet been voted on. A perfect score is one less than the number of items. Items with the least confident scores are prioritized to be part of the next voting pair.
